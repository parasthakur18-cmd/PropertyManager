MENU SLOT LOGIC AUDIT & FIX

Current Problems:

1. "Late Night" is not actually a meal slot for us.
2. We need an "All Day" option instead.
3. Breakfast items are currently visible all day even when Breakfast timing is restricted.
4. Lunch items are also appearing outside Lunch timings.
5. Meal slot timing rules are not being enforced correctly.

Required Changes:

---

1. Replace "Late Night" with "All Day"

Current Slots:

* Breakfast
* Lunch
* High Tea
* Dinner
* Late Night

Change To:

* Breakfast
* Lunch
* High Tea
* Dinner
* All Day

---

2. All Day Logic

If an item is marked:

✅ All Day

Then that item should always be visible regardless of current time.

Examples:

Aloo Paratha → All Day

Visible:

* Breakfast
* Lunch
* High Tea
* Dinner

Any time of day.

---

3. Slot-Based Visibility

If an item is marked only:

✅ Breakfast

Then it should ONLY appear during Breakfast timing.

Example:

Breakfast Time:
07:00 AM → 01:00 PM

Visible:
✅ 07:00 AM
✅ 10:00 AM
✅ 12:30 PM

Hidden:
❌ 01:01 PM
❌ 02:00 PM
❌ 07:00 PM

---

4. Lunch Visibility

If item is marked:

✅ Lunch

Lunch Time:
12:00 PM → 04:00 PM

Visible:
✅ 12:00 PM
✅ 02:00 PM
✅ 03:59 PM

Hidden:
❌ Morning
❌ Evening
❌ Night

---

5. Multiple Slot Logic

Example:

Aloo Paratha

Selected:

✅ Breakfast
✅ Lunch

Visible:

Breakfast Time → Show

Lunch Time → Show

High Tea Time → Hide

Dinner Time → Hide

---

6. All Day Overrides Everything

If:

✅ All Day

Then ignore meal timing restrictions and always show item.

---

7. Menu Filtering Fix

Audit current menu API.

Verify:

Items are filtered based on:

Current Time
AND
Selected Meal Slots

Current bug:

Breakfast items visible all day.
Lunch items visible all day.

Fix this.

---

8. Admin Preview

Add:

"Preview Menu At Time"

Examples:

08:00 AM
01:30 PM
05:00 PM
08:00 PM

Admin can verify exactly what guests will see.

---

Expected Final Behavior

Breakfast Item:
Visible only during Breakfast.

Lunch Item:
Visible only during Lunch.

Dinner Item:
Visible only during Dinner.

All Day Item:
Visible all day.

Meal timings must be strictly enforced for guest ordering.
