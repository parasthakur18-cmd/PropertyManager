Owner BI Enhancement Suggestion – Booking Source Intelligence & Executive Brief

The Revenue Opportunity Dashboard is useful, but currently it only shows occupancy, ARR, unsold nights, and revenue opportunity at the property level.

The biggest gap is that owners cannot identify which booking source is actually driving the business.

Required Enhancement: Booking Source Filter

Add a global filter:

Booking Source

* All Sources
* OTA
* Walk-in
* Direct
* Travel Agent
* Group Booking
* Corporate
* Website Booking
* Repeat Guest
* Other

This filter should update all Owner BI tabs including:

* CEO Summary
* Executive Dashboard
* Property Scorecard
* Monthly Sales
* OTA vs Walk-in
* Revenue Leakage
* Opportunity Dashboard
* Forecast Calculator

---

Group Booking Intelligence

Currently major revenue comes from Group Bookings.

If Group Booking revenue is mixed with Travel Agent or Direct bookings, owners cannot identify:

* Which travel agent generates maximum revenue
* Which groups generate highest ARR
* Which groups generate highest occupancy
* Which travel agents are worth focusing on
* Which travel agents have declining business

Add a dedicated Group Analysis section.

Metrics:

* Total Group Revenue
* Total Group Room Nights
* Group Occupancy Contribution %
* Group ARR
* Average Group Size
* Top 10 Travel Agents
* Top 10 Group Organizers
* Revenue by Travel Agent
* Revenue by Group Name
* Repeat Group Business %

---

Source Contribution Dashboard

Display:

Booking Source | Revenue | Nights | Occupancy % | ARR | Contribution %

Example:

Travel Agent      ₹18,50,000   640 Nights   42%
OTA               ₹9,20,000    320 Nights   21%
Direct            ₹7,40,000    280 Nights   17%
Walk-in           ₹4,10,000    150 Nights   10%
Corporate         ₹2,90,000    110 Nights   7%
Website           ₹1,10,000     35 Nights   3%

This instantly shows what is driving the business.

---

Executive Brief (AI Summary)

Add a "Brief Analysis" button.

When clicked, AI generates a management summary:

Example:

Business Summary:

* Occupancy is 20.5%, significantly below target.
* 78% of revenue is generated from Group and Travel Agent bookings.
* OTA contribution is only 12%, indicating weak online visibility.
* Average ARR is ₹3,754 which is healthy.
* Revenue opportunity of ₹11.2 lakh exists from unsold inventory.
* Focus should be on increasing OTA visibility and converting travel agent demand into repeat direct bookings.

Recommendations:

1. Increase OTA occupancy during weekdays.
2. Re-engage top performing travel agents.
3. Launch direct booking campaigns.
4. Introduce dynamic pricing for weekends.
5. Target occupancy of 35% before increasing room inventory.
